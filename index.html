Welcome to Ohio Valley Antique Mall!

Ohio Valley Antique Mall is Cincinnati’s Largest Multi-Dealer Antique Mall! We have over 550 dealers located throughout our 85,000 sq. ft. facility, complete with our in-store Cafe and Customer Lounge. We offer all kinds of antiques, collectibles, books, furniture, vintage clothing, accessories and more.

Voted Top 10 BEST Places to Shop in Cincinnati by USA Today!

What We Offer

In addition to coming in and checking out our vendors’ merchandise, we offer a variety of other amenities, both in and out of OVAM.

Loading, Shipping, & Delivery

We will help you load your items if necessary. We ask that you please bring blankets, straps, and/or cardboard to safely get your items home. Shipping is available for small and large items. We also have a delivery service. If you’re interested, head on over to our contact page and send us a message.

Personal Shopper

Need help finding that hard to find item? Let Karen, our Personal Shopper help you locate your favorite items. The service is free! Just fill out our contact form letting Karen know what it is you are searching for and she will contact you if she locates it in the mall!

Gift Certificates Available

We offer gift certificates in any amount desired. They can be purchased in store or ordered over the phone. Please call us at (513) 874 7855

Note: We charge $0.49 for shipping when not picked up in the store
